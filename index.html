About

I'm a software engineer focused on web infrastructure and developer experience. Most of my work lives in the open-source ecosystem — build systems, schema validators, and state management libraries that developers depend on every day.

I believe that great DX compounds quietly: small improvements in tooling create outsized leverage across engineering teams over time. That's the layer I care most about — the invisible scaffolding that makes everything else faster and more reliable.

Outside of code, I read papers on compilers and distributed systems, tinker with new language runtimes, and chase the best ramen spot in whatever city I'm in.
